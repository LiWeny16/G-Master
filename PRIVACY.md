# Privacy Policy / 隐私策略

**Effective Date:** 2026-03-28

This privacy policy applies to the **G-Master** browser extension.

## 1. Data Collection & Usage
G-Master operates primarily locally within your browser. 
- **User Prompts and Chat Data**: All chat data between you and the Gemini service remains on the respective platforms. The extension reads the webpage context strictly to enable its features (Deep Think, Multi-turn interactions, System Prompts) and does not upload your conversations to our servers.
- **Tavily Search API**: If you use the search engine capabilities (Tavily), search queries may be routed through the Tavily API strictly for fulfilling your search request.
- **Local Workspace**: Local file access is explicitly granted and controlled by you. Files are not sent to any external server.

## 2. Telemetry and Analytics
We do not collect personal identifiable information (PII), track browsing history outside of the active Gemini app, or use tracking cookies. 

## 3. Storage
Settings and System Prompts you configure within G-Master are saved securely in your browser's local storage (`chrome.storage.local`).

## 4. Updates to this Policy
We may update this policy. We will notify users by updating the effective date of this document.

If you have any questions, please open an issue in our GitHub repository.
